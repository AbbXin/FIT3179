Explore the impact of vaccines on COVID-19

World Map of Vaccination per hundred

In this map of vaccination rates, we can see that vaccination rates in Europe are high, with most countries above 60% and some above 80%, such as Portugal, and vaccination rates in North and South America are also high, with most countries having vaccination rates above 40%. While vaccination rates in Africa are very low, with most African countries having vaccination rates of less than one percent.

World Map of Total Deaths per million

In this map of total deaths per million, comparison by continent, South America has the highest number of Total deaths per million, while Oceania and Africa have the lowest numbers.

Total Deaths VS Number of People Fully Vaccinated

In this graph we see that the number of total deaths and the number of people fully vaccinated are positively correlated, meaning that the more the number of total deaths the country has, the more the number of people fully vaccinated a country has. When you use the filter tool to filter minimum confirmed cases, you will find that total cases are also proportional to total deaths, meaning that as there are more total cases, there will be more total deaths. Also, when selecting continent with the filter tool, we find that all countries in South America have a total deaths greater than 1000.

The change of the Daily Cases & Daily Deaths over time

In this area chart, it shows the daily deaths and daily cases in 8 countries with vaccination rates above 70% (Australia does not have a vaccination rate of 70%, so it is also shown in this chart to give Australians an idea of the situation in Australia). In this graph, you can see the image of daily deaths and daily cases appeared to be symmetrical like a mirror before May 2021. However, after May 2021, the trend of daily deaths and daily cases did not look like a mirror image, and the trend of daily deaths start to decrease, suggesting a positive effect of vaccines on the control of the COVID-19.
